# Towing Go / No-Go Reference

Three independent checks. One CAT scale stop. All three must be green before heading into the high country. Trailer weight projections assumes that the water is only filled up to 20 gal.

| **Goal** | **How to Calculate** | **Projected Value** | **Compare to** | **GO if...** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |--- |
| **1. Truck GVWR** | Steer axle + Drive axle (from CAT scale ticket) | 5,400 lbs | Truck door jamb GVWR (~7,050 lbs for Lariat — verify your sticker) | Steer + Drive axle total is UNDER door jamb GVWR |
| **2. Towing capacity** | Trailer axle × 1.15 (accounts for missing tongue weight) Est. loaded: ~6,500 lbs | 6,167 lbs | Truck towing capacity (~9,000 lbs mountain-derated; spec is 13,200 lbs) See Ford towing guide PDF for your exact config | Estimated trailer weight is UNDER towing capacity |
| **3. Trailer GVWR** | Same as above: Trailer axle × 1.15 Est. loaded: ~6,500 lbs | 6,167 lbs | Trailer sticker GVWR (6,995 lbs for Grand Design 22BHE — fixed number) | Estimated trailer weight is UNDER trailer GVWR |
