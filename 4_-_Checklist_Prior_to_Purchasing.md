# Checklist Prior to Purchasing

* What is the exact payload number on the door jamb sticker for the specific Lariat being ordered?
* Make sure Tow/Haul Package and Max Tow Axle is included.
* Is FordPass phone / Apple Watch remote start free permanently on 2026 trucks, or does it require a subscription after a trial?
* Is wireless charging included?
* Will the Lariat fit in my garage?
* What is the Auto Stop Start situation?
* Is there a backup camera?
* Do the side mirrors have blind spot checking?
* How much space is there in the back?
