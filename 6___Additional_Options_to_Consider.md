# Additional Options to Consider — F-150 Lariat

Options beyond the core towing configuration (3.5EB + Tow/Haul Package + Max Tow Axle).

| Option | Description | Est. Price | Yes / No / Maybe |
|---|---|---|---|
| **TOWING & DRIVING ASSIST** |
| BlueCruise (hands-free highway driving) | Hands-free highway driving on 300,000+ miles of divided highways. Comes equipped on Lariat; activation requires subscription (~$2,305–$2,875 for 7-year one-time purchase, or trial included). | ~$2,305–$2,875 (7-yr plan) | Maybe |
| FX4 Off-Road Package | Off-road tuned shocks, skid plates (fuel tank, transfer case, front differential), Hill Descent Control, Rock Crawl Mode, electronic-locking rear axle. Separate from and stackable with towing config. | ~$1,320 | Maybe |
| **COMFORT & CONVENIENCE** |
| Heated rear seats | Second-row heated seats. Included in Lariat High (502A) equipment group; otherwise not available as a standalone add-on on base Lariat (501A). | Included in 502A upgrade (~$6,880) | Maybe |
| Moonroof (Twin Panel) | Available on Lariat 501A and 502A. SuperCrew only. | ~$1,595–$1,995 (est.) | Maybe |
| Pro Power Onboard (400W) | 120V outlet in cab and bed — turns the truck into a mobile generator. Useful at campsites. Included in Mobile Office Package on 501A. | ~$780 (via Mobile Office Pkg) | Yes |
| Tailgate step | Fold-out step for easy bed access given ride height. Included in Bed Utility Package along with bed storage boxes, work surface, tie-downs, and LED box lighting. | ~$860–$1,845 (via Bed Utility Pkg) | Yes |
| **BED & EXTERIOR** |
| Spray-in bed liner | Permanent spray-on coating (e.g. Line-X, Rhino) that bonds to bed metal. Protects against scratches, dents, and rust. Better long-term than drop-in liner. Dealer or aftermarket shop. | ~$500–$600 | Yes |
| Tonneau cover | Hard or soft cover over the bed. Protects gear from weather, improves fuel economy slightly. Best decided before delivery for proper fitment. | ~$500–$1,500 (aftermarket) | Maybe |
| **TECHNOLOGY** |
| Trailer camera compatibility | Confirms the truck can display a wireless trailer camera feed on the 12" screen — useful for backing the Grand Design into campsites. Verify at order whether included or add-on. | TBD — verify at order | Yes |

> **Notes:**
> - *Pro Power Onboard* and *Tailgate Step* are bundled into packages (Mobile Office and Bed Utility respectively) — you get the full package, not just the individual item.
> - *Heated rear seats* on the base Lariat (501A) are not available as a standalone option; they require moving up to the 502A equipment group.
> - *BlueCruise* hardware comes equipped on the Lariat; the cost above is for the subscription/activation, not hardware.
> - All prices are estimates based on 2026 Ford pricing data and may vary by dealer/configuration.
