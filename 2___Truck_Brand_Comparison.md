> ★ F-150 Lariat remains the strongest all-around match for this use case. Key differentiator vs. competitors: highest tow ceiling with the best built-in towing tech at this price point. If long-term reliability and resale are top priorities, the Toyota Tundra Limited with iForce MAX hybrid is the compelling alternative — particularly given its altitude advantage (electric motor torque is unaffected at 7,000+ ft). RAM 1500 Laramie offers the best ride and longest warranty but lowest tow ceiling of the group.
